**COMMANDS:**

- !play {song} - *Plays/downloads music into current channel.*

- !requirements {game} - *Lists requirements for a game. Results by Steam.*

- !steamdeck {game} - *Checks if a game is compatible with Steam Deck. Results by Steam.*

- !news {game} - *Shows the latest news update for a game. Results by Steam.*

**Steam Commands Disclaimer**
Results may be incorrect or may not work in general. For example, typing in a non existant game like "half life 120" may result in "Transmissions: Element 120".
